# The LLM Council Prompt

## Get Better Answers From Claude Using Multiple Expert Perspectives

Most people ask Claude one question and get one answer.

The problem?

A single answer often misses blind spots, risks, and better opportunities.

Instead, use the LLM Council Method inspired by the idea of having multiple viewpoints challenge and improve a decision before taking action.

## How It Works

Instead of answering immediately, Claude creates a council of advisors.

Each advisor looks at the problem from a completely different angle.

Then a Chairman reviews everything and produces a final recommendation.

## The Five Advisors

### Advisor 1: The Contrarian

Your job is to find flaws.

Focus on:

*   Risks
*   Weak assumptions
*   Failure scenarios
*   Hidden dangers

Question everything.

Do not be optimistic.

### Advisor 2: First Principles Thinker

Break the problem down to fundamentals.

Ignore:

*   Industry norms
*   Conventional wisdom
*   Existing assumptions

Rebuild the solution from scratch.

### Advisor 3: Expansionist

Your job is to find opportunities.

Focus on:

*   Bigger outcomes
*   Hidden leverage
*   New business models
*   Growth opportunities

Think bigger than everyone else.

### Advisor 4: Outsider

You know nothing about this industry.

Look at the problem with completely fresh eyes.

Challenge things that insiders take for granted.

Suggest unconventional solutions.

### Advisor 5: Executor

You only care about implementation.

Focus on:

*   What to do next
*   Priorities
*   Resources required
*   Action plans

Convert ideas into execution.

## The Chairman

After all advisors finish:

*   Review every perspective
*   Identify agreements
*   Identify disagreements
*   Resolve conflicts
*   Produce the best final recommendation

The final answer must include:

*   Key insights
*   Major risks
*   Biggest opportunities
*   Clear next actions

## Master Prompt

```
Act as an LLM Council.

I want five advisors to analyze my problem before providing a final answer.

Advisor 1: Contrarian

Find flaws, risks, weak assumptions and reasons this may fail.

Advisor 2: First Principles Thinker

Ignore conventional wisdom and rebuild the solution from first principles.

Advisor 3: Expansionist

Identify larger opportunities, leverage points and upside scenarios.

Advisor 4: Outsider

Analyze the problem as someone completely unfamiliar with the industry.

Advisor 5: Executor

Create a practical action plan focused on implementation.

After all advisors finish:

Act as Chairman.

Review every viewpoint.

Identify agreements and disagreements.

Resolve conflicts.

Provide a final recommendation.

Finish with:

- Top 3 Risks

- Top 3 Opportunities

- Next 7 Days Action Plan

- Final Verdict

My problem is:

[INSERT PROBLEM HERE]
```

### Career Decisions

## Power Upgrade

Add this line at the end:

`Challenge each other's reasoning before the Chairman provides the final verdict.`

This forces the advisors to debate instead of simply giving independent answers.

The result is usually a much deeper and more balanced response.

## Final Takeaway

Most people use AI to get answers.

Top users use AI to challenge their thinking.

The quality of your decisions often depends on the quality of the perspectives you consider before acting.
